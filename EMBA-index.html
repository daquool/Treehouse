Georgetown University’s McDonough School of Business offers a world-class Executive MBA (EMBA) that accommodates the fast-paced schedule of today’s busy executive.

  • EMBA Curriculum: The dynamic 20-month curriculum offers a personal and professional transformational experience that emphasizes a global business focus and Georgetown’s presence in Washington, D.C.

  • Global Experiences: All students engage in two international residencies during which they provide consulting services to Fortune 500 companies abroad and study the impact of globalization in a foreign country at the national, industry, and firm levels.

  • Career Development: Georgetown McDonough is committed to helping EMBA students retool, maximize their high potential, and cultivate greater stature as business leaders through career seminars, coaching, and executive alumni events.
